Every celebration deserves something sweet, fresh, and unforgettable.

Donel Bakes offers a mouthwatering selection of cakes and snacks to suit every taste. Our cake collection includes three signature flavors:

  • Chocolate Cake Rich, moist, and perfect for chocolate lovers.
  • Vanilla Cake A classic favorite, smooth and delicately sweet.
  • Coconut Cake Light and tropical, made with real coconut.

For snack lovers, we feature:

  • Biscuits : "Zote Zote" A delightful assortment of crunchy biscuits, ideal for any time of day.
  • Ubuyu Traditional sweet tamarind seeds, bursting with flavor and nostalgia.

Everything is made fresh to order with careful attention to taste and quality.

Why Choose Donel Bakes

There’s a reason our customers keep coming back. At Donel Bakes, we don’t compromise on quality. Every item is baked fresh using only high-grade ingredients. We also provide custom cake orders, perfect for birthdays, weddings, or any special event. On top of that, we take pride in our on-time delivery, affordable pricing, and friendly, helpful service. With us, you’re not just buying food—you’re experiencing genuine care and culinary craftsmanship.

Fast, Friendly Delivery

We make it easy to enjoy our delicious offerings wherever you are. Our dedicated delivery team covers popular routes from Arusha to Dar es Salaam and Arusha to Moshi. Orders are prepared and dispatched within 48 hours to guarantee freshness and prompt arrival. At Donel Bakes, we understand the value of your time, and we’re committed to delivering happiness directly to your doorstep.

Where to Find Us

Our kitchen is located in Boma Ng’ombe, Kilimanjaro, where every cake and snack is carefully prepared. This central location allows us to serve both local customers and long-distance routes efficiently. You're welcome to contact us or visit us to place orders or learn more about our products.

Special Promotions, Affordable Prices

We love rewarding our customers with great deals! At Donel Bakes, we regularly offer promotional pricing to make our products even more accessible. Whether you're ordering a single cake or a snack bundle, we ensure that you receive excellent value. Check in with us to learn about our current offers and seasonal discounts.
